About Sociofuzz

Movie Ratings, Reviews and Analysis from Social Media

Movies with social media

SocioFuzz provides high quality information and insights about a movie at single place by mashing data from various streams like facebook, twitter, imdb, wikipedia and youtube to provide compact social opinions about the movies.


Data oriented insights and trends

Our backend algorithms and technologies performs number crunching on the data using Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning Algorithms to predict mood of the social sphere. The insights are rigorously examined and presented in a ready to pick manner backed by unbiased stats.


Social media Fuzz : The rating algorithm

The Sociofuzz Rating for a movie is calculated using statistics applied on social media data, whic takes care of various aspects like sentiment analysis, reach and influence, collated critic ratings and popularity buzz.

People rely on ratings before deciding to spend bucks for a matinee show.

However most portals are backed by regular users and critics, hence opting out significant opinions of people who refrain to cast their votes
